I have been operating this website since 2008. It began as a simple portfolio, which was used to help land my first job out of college. Over the years it has evolved between being a portfolio, blog, code playground, and everything in between.

The most recent iteration of this site is an attempt to unify some of these previous purposes, while giving me an opportunity to play around with Jamstack-style development. Admittedly, it's a bit corporate-looking at the moment. The plan is to infuse it with a bit more personality as time goes on.

Check back every now and then if you want to join the journey, or keep reading to learn more about the nitty-gritty details.
